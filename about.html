About the Author

Hello, my name is Joseph Nagy. I am a senior Computer Science Engineering student at Michigan State University and I enjoy building for the web on the front-end. I have been creating websites, specifically for Municipalities and small businesses, for over a year now and some of the clients I have worked with include the City of DeWitt,NATaT, and the Oldtown General Store.

Some of my technical skills include:

  • Front-end frameworks - Angular, Flask
  • Programming Languages - TypeScript/JavaScript, PHP, HTML/CSS, Python, C++
  • Tools - Visual Studio Code, WordPress, Dreamweaver, Figma

In my free time, I enjoy Mountain Biking, Rock Climbing, playing Valorant/Overwatch, and doing random fun things with my family.
